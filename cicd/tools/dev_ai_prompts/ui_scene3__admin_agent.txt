4) Derived Metrics (JS functions to implement)

conversionRate = conversions / sessions

containmentRate = 1 - (escalations / sessions)

avgSentiment = mean(sentiment_score)

csat = mean(feedback_rating where not null)

avgResponseMs = mean(first_response_ms)

modelCostTotal = sum(model_cost_usd)

revenueTotal = sum(conversion_value_usd)

roas = revenueTotal / modelCostTotal (guard divide-by-zero)

FAQ Coverage Gap Score per intent:
gap = volume * (1 - avgConfidence) * max(0, 0.3 - avgSentiment)
Use to populate FAQ suggestions.

Purchase Signal heuristic:
(intent in ["pricing","buy","checkout","demo_booked"] ? 1 : 0) + (sentiment>0 ? 0.2:0) + (email_captured=="yes"?0.3:0).

5) SMB-Centric Insight Logic (examples)

Which products should we promote?
Show intents with high sentiment + high conversion rate; recommend ad headlines from top prompts.

Where are customers getting stuck?
Landing pages with high sessions, low confidence, high escalations.

What new FAQs/content will reduce support load?
Top gap intents with sample questions (from prompt_text), plus suggested page to place them.

Are campaigns bringing qualified traffic?
Compare campaign_source/medium by conversion rate, cost/session, ROAS.

Is our assistant fast enough?
Correlate first_response_ms with CSAT and conversion rate.

Each card should include an estimated business impact (e.g., “Addressing shipping info could reduce escalations by ~12–18% based on last 30 days.”) using simple multipliers from current data.
